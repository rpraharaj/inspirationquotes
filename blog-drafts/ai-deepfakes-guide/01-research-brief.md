## Detection Red Flags

### Visual
1. Unnatural eye movements/blinking
2. Distorted/inconsistent teeth
3. Unnatural skin texture (too smooth/too wrinkly)
4. Lack of microexpressions
5. Lip-sync inconsistencies
6. Lighting/shadow inconsistencies
7. Flickering edges or blurring
8. Incoherent/illegible text in images

### Audio
- Unnaturally flat voices
- Lack of variation in tone
- Artificial background noise
- Uniform patterns in cloned voices
