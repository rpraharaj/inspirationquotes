## Key Data Points (2026 Current)

### Top AI Tools for Graphic Designers
1. **Adobe Photoshop 2026** - Generative Fill 2.0, Harmonize tool, AI upscaling
2. **Adobe Firefly 3** - Production workhorse for vector/texture/typography
3. **Canva Magic Studio** - Magic Resize, Magic Write, Magic Edit, brand kits
4. **Midjourney v7** - Visual imagination, moodboards, concept art
5. **Figma AI** - UI/UX co-pilot, auto-layout, wireframe generation
6. **Runway ML (Gen-3 Alpha)** - Motion graphics, video clips
7. **Khroma AI** - Color intelligence, personalized palettes
8. **Kittl AI** - Typography, logos, vintage styles
9. **Uizard** - Sketches to wireframes
10. **ChatGPT + DALL-E** - Ideation, concept generation

### Key Statistics
- AI automates repetitive tasks: background removal, resizing, color correction
- Focus shift: designers now spend more time on strategy and creativity
- AI-human collaboration is the 2026 standard

### What AI Does Well
- Background removal
- Image resizing for multiple platforms
- Color palette generation
- Layout suggestions
- Design variation generation
- Moodboard creation
- Quick prototyping

### What AI Still Needs Humans For
- Strategic thinking
- Emotional intelligence in design
- Client relationships
- Brand storytelling
- Original concept development
- Cultural context
- Final polish and refinement
