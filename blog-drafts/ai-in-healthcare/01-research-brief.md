## 4. Current AI Healthcare Landscape (as of January 2026)

### Key Technologies in Use
- **Ambient listening/documentation** - Auto-transcribes patient encounters
- **Medical imaging AI** - Analyzes X-rays, MRIs, CT scans
- **Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS)** - Real-time diagnostic assistance
- **Predictive analytics** - Early warning for sepsis, falls, readmissions
- **Natural Language Processing** - Extracts data from clinical notes
- **Generative AI** - Clinical documentation, drug discovery

### Major Shifts in 2025-2026
- Enterprise-scale deployment replacing pilot programs
- EHR vendors building native AI capabilities
- Agentic AI emerging for autonomous problem-solving
- Focus on transparency and ethical governance
- AI addressing physician burnout and workforce shortages
