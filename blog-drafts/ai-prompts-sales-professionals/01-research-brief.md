## 15. Voice and Tone Guidelines

### Writing Style for This Post

**Tone:** Professional but conversational, optimistic about AI as a sales tool

**Voice Characteristics:**
- **Empathetic:** "I know cold calling is brutal. Here's how AI can help..."
- **Practical:** "Here's the exact prompt I use for follow-ups..."
- **Honest:** "This won't work for every prospect. Use your judgment."
- **Energetic:** "Sales just got 10x easier. Here's how..."

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Hype without substance ("AI will make you a millionaire!")
- ❌ Overly technical jargon (keep it accessible)
- ❌ Generic AI clichés ("In today's fast-paced world...")
- ❌ Claiming AI replaces human skills

**Use:**
- ✅ Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- ✅ Bullet points and lists (scannable)
- ✅ Specific numbers ("This prompt cut my research time from 30 min to 5 min")
- ✅ Real-world scenarios ("Imagine you're calling a CFO who says 'too expensive'...")
