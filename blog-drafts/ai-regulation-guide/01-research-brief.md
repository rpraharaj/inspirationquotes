## 2. Key Regulations to Cover

### EU AI Act (Primary Focus)
- Implemented in phases: 2025-2027
- Risk-based approach: unacceptable, high, limited, minimal risk
- Key dates:
  - Feb 2, 2025: Prohibited practices banned
  - Aug 2, 2025: GPAI model obligations
  - Aug 2, 2026: High-risk AI requirements fully applicable
- Penalties: Up to €35 million or 7% global revenue

### US AI Regulation
- No single federal law (patchwork approach)
- State laws: California, Colorado, Texas, Illinois leading
- Key areas: employment AI, transparency, bias prevention
- Trump Executive Order Dec 2025: pro-innovation stance

### Key Requirements
- AI literacy requirements
- Transparency and disclosure
- Bias prevention and fairness
- Risk assessments for high-risk AI
- Technical documentation
- Human oversight
