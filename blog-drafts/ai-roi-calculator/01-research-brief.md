## Key Research Points

- Most businesses don't measure AI ROI properly
- Framework: Scope → Costs → Benefits → Calculate → Decide
- Include: direct costs, implementation costs, training costs, hidden costs
- Benefits: time savings, cost avoidance, revenue impact, quality improvements
- Typical healthy ROI: 100-500%
