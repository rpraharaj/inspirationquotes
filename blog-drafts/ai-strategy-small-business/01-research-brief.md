**Created:** 2026-01-09
**Primary Keyword:** AI strategy small business
**Category:** ai-business

## Key Research Points

- Small businesses need focused AI adoption, not wholesale transformation
- Start with pain points, not tools
- 2026 trend: AI moving from buzzword to strategic necessity
- Best starting tools: ChatGPT, Claude (general purpose)
- ROI typically visible within weeks for simple implementations
- Training and change management critical for adoption
