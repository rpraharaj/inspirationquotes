### H2: FAQ (300 words)

#### H3: Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does it take to become a prompt engineer?**
A: With focused effort, 6-12 months. Some people land roles in 3 months with strong backgrounds; others take 18+ months while working full-time jobs.

**Q: Do you need a degree to be a prompt engineer?**
A: No. Most employers care about demonstrated skills, not degrees. A strong portfolio matters more than a CS degree for this specific role.

**Q: Is prompt engineering a real job in 2026?**
A: Yes, but the title is evolving. Many "prompt engineering" functions are being absorbed into broader AI roles. The skill is real and valuable; the standalone title may become less common.

**Q: Can I become a prompt engineer without coding?**
A: You can start without coding, but basic Python will significantly expand your opportunities. Focus on learning fundamentals, not becoming a software engineer.

**Q: What's the best free resource to start learning?**
A: Google Prompting Essentials (free), DeepLearning.AI ChatGPT Prompt Engineering course, and hands-on practice with ChatGPT/Claude free tiers.

**Q: Will AI automate prompt engineering jobs?**
A: AI is improving at basic prompting, but complex use cases still need human expertise. The role will evolve toward more strategic, specialized work rather than disappear.
