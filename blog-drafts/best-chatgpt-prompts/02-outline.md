### H2: Frequently Asked Questions (300-400 words)

**Q1: What makes a ChatGPT prompt "good"?**
- Clear, specific, with context and desired format

**Q2: Do longer prompts get better results?**
- Not necessarily - clarity matters more than length
- GPT-5 handles context better, so focus on relevance

**Q3: Can I use these prompts with other AI tools?**
- Yes, most work with Claude, Gemini, and other LLMs
- May need minor adjustments

**Q4: How do I know if a prompt is working?**
- Evaluate: Does it give consistent, useful results?
- Test multiple times with variations

**Q5: Should I use role-based prompts (Act as...)?**
- Yes, assigning roles improves output quality
- Especially useful for specialized tasks
