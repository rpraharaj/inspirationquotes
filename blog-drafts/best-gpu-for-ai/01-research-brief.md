## 2. Current GPU Landscape (January 2026)

### NVIDIA GPUs
- **RTX 5090** - 32GB VRAM, Blackwell architecture, ~$1999, 213 tokens/sec on 8B models
- **RTX 4090** - 24GB VRAM, still excellent choice, ~$1599
- **RTX 3090** (used) - 24GB VRAM, best value for VRAM, ~$600-800 used
- **RTX 4070 Ti Super** - 16GB VRAM, mid-range option
- **RTX 4060 Ti 16GB** - Budget option for serious development
- **RTX 6000 Ada** - 48GB VRAM, professional grade

### AMD GPUs
- **RX 9000 series (RDNA 4)** - 4x more AI compute than RDNA 3
- **Radeon AI Pro R9700** - 32GB VRAM, workstation
- **RX 7900 XTX** - 24GB VRAM, ROCm compatibility

### Key Requirements
- **VRAM:** Primary factor - 2GB per billion parameters (FP16)
- **8GB minimum:** 7B models with 4-bit quantization
- **12GB better:** 7B+ at higher precision
- **24GB+:** For 34B-70B+ models
- **Memory Bandwidth:** 600+ GB/s recommended
