## Key Findings

### Prompting Best Practices
- Be specific: tone, style, audience, goals
- Define format and length explicitly
- Use role-playing ("Act as a...")
- Set constraints and exclusions
- Iterative refinement

### Use Cases
- Ideation and brainstorming
- Content repurposing
- SEO optimization
- Grammar and editing
- Content calendars

### Important Considerations
- AI augments, not replaces human creativity
- Always maintain brand voice
- Human oversight essential
- Verify facts and claims
