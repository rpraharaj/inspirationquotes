## 9. Common Mistakes to Address

1. **Vague instructions** - Not being specific enough about behavior
2. **Outdated knowledge files** - Not updating uploaded documents
3. **Ignoring tone/personality** - Generic responses without character
4. **Too many capabilities enabled** - Confusion about what to use
5. **No conversation starters** - Users don't know how to begin
6. **Overloading with files** - Too many documents causing confusion
7. **Not testing thoroughly** - Publishing without iteration
8. **Privacy concerns** - Uploading sensitive business data
