- Definition: Unified system vs point solutions

  - Integration capabilities
- Brief history: Evolution from pilot projects to strategic infrastructure

### H2: 10 Essential Criteria for Evaluating Enterprise AI Platforms (800 words)
*Target for featured snippet - numbered list*

1. **Integration Capabilities** - APIs, connectors, ecosystem compatibility
2. **Scalability** - Growth capacity, performance under load
3. **Security & Compliance** - Encryption, certifications, data residency
4. **AI Model Flexibility** - Multi-model support, fine-tuning options
5. **Data Handling** - Structured/unstructured, governance, quality
6. **Ease of Deployment** - Cloud, on-prem, hybrid options
7. **User Experience** - Interface, training requirements
8. **Vendor Reliability** - Stability, support, roadmap
9. **Total Cost of Ownership** - All-in pricing, hidden costs
10. **Explainability & Transparency** - Audit trails, bias detection
