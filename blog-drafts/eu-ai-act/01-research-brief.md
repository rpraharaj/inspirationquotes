# Research Brief: EU AI Act: What It Means for AI Development

**Created:** 2026-01-10
**Primary Keyword:** EU AI Act
**Search Intent:** Informational
**Target Audience:** Developers, businesses, and tech professionals affected by EU AI regulations

---

## Key Dates

- Aug 1, 2024: Act entered into force
- Feb 2, 2025: Prohibited AI practices banned, AI literacy requirements
- Aug 2, 2025: GPAI obligations effective
- Aug 2, 2026: Most high-risk provisions effective

## Risk Categories
1. Unacceptable (Banned)
2. High-Risk (Strict requirements)
3. Limited (Transparency obligations)
4. Minimal (No requirements)

## Penalties
- Up to €35M or 7% global turnover for prohibited practices
- Up to €15M or 3% for other violations
