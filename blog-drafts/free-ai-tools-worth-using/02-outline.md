### H2: Best Free AI Chatbots (4 Tools) (800 words)
**Goal:** Cover the foundational conversational AI tools

#### H3: 1. ChatGPT Free — The Gold Standard (Still)
**Word Count:** 200 words
**Content:**
- What it is: OpenAI's flagship, GPT-4o mini on free tier
- Free limits: ~50 messages/day, file uploads, image generation limited
- Best for: General queries, writing help, coding assistance
- Gotcha: Rate limits hit fast during peak hours
- My take: "Still the first tool I reach for, despite limits"

#### H3: 2. Google Gemini — Best for Research
**Word Count:** 200 words
**Content:**
- What it is: Google's multimodal AI, integrated with Google ecosystem
- Free limits: Generous, no hard daily cap for basic use
- Best for: Research, Google Docs integration, image analysis
- No signup required: Can use directly in browser
- Gotcha: Occasionally refuses to answer "controversial" topics
- My take: "Underrated because everyone's stuck on ChatGPT"

#### H3: 3. Microsoft Copilot — GPT-4 for Free
**Word Count:** 200 words
**Content:**
- What it is: Microsoft's AI, powered by GPT-4 (yes, the paid model)
- Free limits: Generous with Microsoft account
- Best for: Windows users, Office integration, image generation with DALL-E 3
- No signup required: Works in Edge browser
- Gotcha: Responses can be overly cautious
- My take: "Best free access to GPT-4, period"

#### H3: 4. HuggingChat — The Open-Source Champion
**Word Count:** 200 words
**Content:**
- What it is: Open-source chatbot with access to multiple models
- Free limits: Completely free, no limits
- Best for: Privacy-conscious users, developers, trying different models
- Gotcha: UI less polished, some models less capable
- My take: "Perfect for when you don't trust Big Tech with your data"
