1. **Deep Think Mode** - Advanced reasoning for complex problems (math, science, logic)
2. **Enhanced Multimodality** - Text, images, audio, video, code, 3D objects processing
3. **Massive Context Window** - 1M tokens (Flash), 2M+ (Pro) - entire books, codebases
4. **Agentic Workflows** - Autonomous coding, multi-step task execution
5. **Google Ecosystem Integration** - Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Maps, Search, Android XR
