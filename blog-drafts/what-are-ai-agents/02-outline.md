### H2: Frequently Asked Questions
**Words:** 400-450
**Goal:** Capture PAA traffic, provide quick answers

**Questions:**

1. **What is the difference between an AI agent and a chatbot?**
   Answer focus: Chatbots respond to queries; AI agents take actions to complete goals autonomously. Agents have memory, use tools, and can execute multi-step workflows.

2. **Can AI agents operate without human oversight?**
   Answer focus: Modern agents operate within boundaries you set. You control what tools they access, what actions they can take, and when they escalate to humans.

3. **What are the main components of an AI agent?**
   Answer focus: Brain (LLM), perception (inputs), memory (context storage), tools (action capabilities), and planning (goal decomposition).

4. **Are AI agents dangerous or can they "go rogue"?**
   Answer focus: Agents work within defined parameters. However, like any automation, thoughtful guardrails are essential.

5. **What's the simplest way to try an AI agent?**
   Answer focus: Custom GPTs, Microsoft Copilot, or tools like Zapier's AI automation give a taste of agent-like behavior without coding.

6. **How much does it cost to use AI agents?**
   Answer focus: Ranges from free tiers (limited) to enterprise pricing. Frameworks like LangChain are open-source; costs are primarily LLM API usage.
