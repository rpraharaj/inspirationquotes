## 2. Key Concepts to Explain

### What LLMs Are
- AI models designed to understand and generate human-like text
- "Digital brain" for language processing
- Trained on massive datasets (trillions of tokens)
- Have billions to trillions of parameters

### How LLMs Work
1. **Transformer Architecture** - Self-attention mechanisms
2. **Tokenization** - Converting text to numbers
3. **Training** - Pre-training on vast data
4. **Fine-tuning** - RLHF for alignment
5. **Generation** - Predicting next token

### Current Leading LLMs (2026)
- OpenAI GPT-5.x
- Anthropic Claude 4.x
- Google Gemini 3.x
- Meta LLaMA 4.x
- Mistral (lightweight)
