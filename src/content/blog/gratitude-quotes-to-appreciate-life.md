Years ago, I used to wake up with a heavy sense of "not enough." Not enough time, not enough success, not enough energy. It was a tiring way to live. Everything changed when I started a simple practice of writing down three things I was thankful for every morning. It didn't happen overnight, but slowly, my lens shifted from what was missing to what was already there.

I've found that gratitude isn't just a feeling; it's a muscle we choose to flex. These quotes helped me when I was struggling to find the light, and I hope they do the same for you. There is something magical about finding the right words at the right moment to shift your perspective.

Here are 150 powerful gratitude quotes to help you appreciate life, even on the days when it feels a little bit harder to do so.
