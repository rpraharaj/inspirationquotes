I've spent many late nights staring at the ceiling, wondering if I was just "going through the motions." We’ve all been there—the feeling that life is happening *to* us rather than *through* us. A few years ago, during a particularly directionless season of my life, I started collecting words that felt like compass needles. These quotes about meaning and purpose didn't just give me comfort; they gave me a roadmap.

When I first started searching for my "why," I thought it would be a lightning bolt moment. I expected to wake up one Tuesday and suddenly know exactly what I was meant to do for the next fifty years. But as these quotes taught me, purpose is often less about a destination and more about the quality of our attention to the present moment. It's the "Great Perhaps" we're all seeking, as François Rabelais so beautifully put it.

In this collection, I’ve curated 150 of the most profound quotes on meaning and purpose that have helped me—and millions of others—reconnect with what truly matters. Whether you're in a transition period or just need a daily reminder of your worth, I hope these words spark the same flame in you that they did in me.
