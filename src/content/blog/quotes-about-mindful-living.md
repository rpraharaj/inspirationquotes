Last Tuesday, I found myself standing in my kitchen, staring at my empty coffee mug, and I realized I had absolutely no memory of drinking it. I’d been so busy scrolling through emails and mentally planning my 10:00 AM meeting that I completely missed the one moment of peace I’d set aside for myself.
