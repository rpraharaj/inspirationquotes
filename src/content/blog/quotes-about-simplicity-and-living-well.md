A few years ago, I found myself sitting in a room filled with "success." I had the gadgets, the overstuffed bookshelf, and a calendar so packed I needed a second app just to manage the notifications. Yet, despite having everything I thought I wanted, I felt incredibly heavy. My mind was as cluttered as my desk, and the constant noise of modern life was drowning out the things that actually mattered.

That's when I stumbled across a simple realization: more isn't always better; often, it’s just more. I started stripping away the excess—first the physical clutter, then the digital noise, and finally the mental habits that were keeping me exhausted. In the quiet that followed, I found a level of contentment I hadn't known was possible.

I've found that simplicity isn't about deprivation or living in a sterile box. It’s about making room for what you love by removing everything that distracts you from it. These quotes have been my guideposts during that transition, reminding me that a "good life" is often a simple one.

Here is a collection of 150 quotes about simplicity and living well that have helped me refocus on what truly matters. I hope they offer you the same clarity and peace they’ve given me.
