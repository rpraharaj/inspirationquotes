## What is a Soulmate? Quotes on Deep Connection

The word "soulmate" gets thrown around a lot, but what does it actually mean? For me, it's that rare person who hears the music in your heart even when you've forgotten the tune. It's a connection that transcends the physical and taps into something much deeper. When I first started exploring this idea, I realized that a soulmate isn't just a lover, but a mirror that shows you who you truly are.

1. > "A soulmate is someone who has locks that fit our keys, and keys to fit our locks." — Richard Bach

2. > "Our souls already know each other, don't they?' he whispered. 'It's our bodies that are new.'" — Karen Ross
