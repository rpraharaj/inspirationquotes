## Living a Life of Daily Gratitude

I hope these 150 short quotes about gratitude serve as a reminder that thankfulness isn’t a grand gesture reserved for big occasions. It is a quiet, steady practice that we can carry with us into every room we enter and every conversation we have.

I've found that the best way to use these quotes is to pick one each morning—just one—and let it be your "lens" for the day. If you choose "Enough is a feast," look for all the ways you have "enough" throughout your afternoon. If you choose "Gratitude is the memory of the heart," spend a few minutes thinking about someone who helped you years ago.

Gratitude doesn't make the challenges of life disappear, but it does make them easier to carry. It reminds us that we are part of something bigger, that we are supported, and that even on our hardest days, there is always, always something to be thankful for.
